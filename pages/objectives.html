Aims & Objectives

The core principles and institutional goals that guide every initiative of the BCE Alumni Welfare Association since its inception.

Mission Statements

What We Stand For

Our objectives are designed to foster professional growth, student welfare, and lifelong institutional bonds.

Global Networking

Creating a vibrant and active platform for the networking of BCE alumni based across the globe.

Student Welfare

Actively promoting the welfare of current students of BCE Bhagalpur through mentorship and financial support.

Institution Connect

Bridging the gap between the industry and the institution by sharing opportunities and career insights.

Advocacy

Helping our Almamater achieve status as a top engineering destination through technical involvement.

Actionable Goals

Core Commitments

Comprehensive strategies that underpin the work of the association.

01

Unite Graduates

Uniting graduates of BCE under one dynamic network and encouraging regional chapters worldwide.

02

Spirit of Brotherhood

Fostering friendly feelings and lifelong solidarity among alumni members globally.

03

Upholding dignity and social responsibility, encouraging alumni to contribute meaningfully.

04

Acting as a catalyst for professional development and supporting members in distress.

Managing and investing association funds transparently to achieve long-term goals.

06

Providing a dedicated safety net and assistance for the families of members in need.

07

Assisting BCE in becoming a premier engineering destination through consistent advocacy.

08

Student Mentorship

Actively shaping student careers as future leaders and providing direct financial scholarships.
