Aims & Objectives

The core principles and institutional goals that guide every initiative of the BCE Alumni Welfare Association since its inception.

Mission Statements

What We Stand For

Our objectives are designed to foster professional growth, student welfare, and lifelong institutional bonds.

Global Networking

Creating a vibrant and active platform for the networking of BCE alumni based across the globe, starting from our hub in Bangalore.

Student Welfare

Actively promoting the welfare of current students of BCE Bhagalpur through professional mentorship and financial support.

Campus Connect

Bridging the gap between the industry and the institution by sharing opportunities and career insights through our flagship programs.

Industry Excellence

Helping our Almamater achieve status as a top engineering college through technical advocacy and active involvement.

Actionable Goals

Core Commitments

Comprehensive strategies that underpin the work of the association.

01

Unite Graduates

Uniting graduates of BCE under one dynamic network and encouraging regional chapters worldwide.

02

Spirit of Brotherhood

Fostering friendly feelings and lifelong solidarity among members of the association globally.

03

Ethical Responsibility

Upholding dignity and social responsibility, encouraging alumni to contribute meaningfully to society.

04

Professional Growth

Acting as a catalyst for professional development and supporting members during times of distress.

05

Resource Management

Managing and investing association funds transparently to achieve our long-term goals.

06

Family Welfare

Providing a dedicated safety net and assistance for the families of association members in need.

07

Institutional Elevation

Assisting BCE in becoming a premier engineering destination through consistent advocacy.

08

Student Mentorship

Actively shaping student careers as future leaders and providing direct financial scholarships.
