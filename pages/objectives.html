Aims & Objectives

The principles and goals that guide every initiative of the BCE Alumni Welfare Association.

High-Level Goals

What We Stand For

Following are the high-level objectives of the association, guiding our work in Bangalore and beyond.

Networking Platform in Bangalore

Creating a vibrant and active platform for the networking of BCE students and alumni based in Bangalore.

Welfare of BCE Students

Actively promoting the welfare of current students of BCE Bhagalpur through support, resources, and community engagement.

Campus Connect — Mentor & Support

Mentoring and supporting college students through our flagship Campus Connect programme — guiding them towards a successful future.

Closer to Industry

Bringing students closer to industry by sharing opportunities, job openings, and real-world career insights from our alumni network.

In Particular

Detailed Objectives

A comprehensive set of commitments underpinning the work of the BCE Alumni Welfare Association.

01

Unite Graduates & Build a Dynamic Network

To organise and unite the graduates of BCE and to develop a dynamic network of all alumni, bringing all under one umbrella. To encourage the formation of chapters to increase participation of alumni across geographies.

02

Promote Brotherhood & Solidarity

To promote friendly feelings and foster the spirit of brotherhood and solidarity among members of the association and others, strengthening lifelong bonds between BCEians.

03

Uphold Ethical Values & Social Responsibility

To promote a sense of ethical values, dignity and responsibility to the society and nation — encouraging alumni to use their skills and influence for the greater good.

04

Facilitate Professional Growth & Member Support

To act as a facilitator in the professional growth of the members of the association and extend due support in the event of distress to members — ensuring no BCEian stands alone.

05

Fundraising & Financial Management

Raising funds and donations to achieve the aims and objectives of the association and to further invest and manage the funds and money of the association in a responsible and transparent manner.

06

Welfare Organisation for Needy Families

To develop as a welfare organisation to support the needy family members of the association, providing a safety net for those who require assistance during difficult times.

07

Elevate BCE to a Top Engineering College

To help Alma Mater achieve the status of a top engineering college of the country — through advocacy, contributions, and active involvement in the institution's development.

08

Mentor Students & Support Future Leaders

To act as mentor for the students of the college and facilitate them in shaping their careers as future leaders — and to extend financial support to economically weaker students of the college.
