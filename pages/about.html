About Us

Learn about our institution, our association, and the initiatives that connect generations of BCEians.

Est. 1960

BCE Alumni Welfare Association

The BCE Alumni Welfare Association is the official body representing all graduates of Bhagalpur College of Engineering. Founded to nurture lifelong connections among our alumni, the association works tirelessly to support its members and contribute to the institution's legacy.

With members spanning six decades of engineering education across India and around the world, we are more than an alumni network — we are a family united by our shared experience, values, and pride in our alma mater.

Our association actively organizes events, facilitates networking, supports students still on campus, and advocates for the welfare of all BCE graduates — wherever they may be in the world.

Our Institution

Bhagalpur College of Engineering

BCE

Bhagalpur College of Engineering (BCE), located in the historic city of Bhagalpur, Bihar, is one of the oldest and most respected engineering institutions in the region. Known for its academic excellence and strong foundation in technical education, BCE has produced generations of skilled engineers who have made valuable contributions across industries in India and abroad. Bhagalpur itself, often referred to as the "Silk City," is rich in culture, heritage, and educational significance.

Our Purpose

What We Do

Building a Connected Community

The Alumni Association of BCE aims to build a strong and connected community of former students, especially creating a vibrant networking platform for BCE alumni in Bangalore. The association is committed to the welfare and growth of current students by fostering mentorship, guidance, and meaningful engagement.

Bridging Academia & Industry

By bringing alumni and students closer to industry opportunities, the association strives to bridge the gap between academic learning and professional growth, ensuring that BCE students are well-supported, inspired, and prepared for their future careers.

Our Flagship Programme

Campus Connect Programme

Campus Connect is designed to bring alumni and current BCE students together through structured mentorship, career guidance sessions, and direct industry exposure.

Through this programme, experienced alumni share real-world insights, help students navigate career choices, and open doors to internship and placement opportunities — ensuring no BCEian enters the professional world unprepared.
