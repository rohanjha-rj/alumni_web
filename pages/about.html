Our Legacy

Bhagalpur College of Engineering Alumni Association — Fostering excellence through community since 1960.

Bridging Generations

The BCE Alumni Welfare Association is more than just a network. It is a prestigious family of engineers who have shaped industries across the globe.

Founded to nurture lifelong connections, our association works tirelessly to celebrate the accomplishments of our members while giving back to the institution that gave us our start.

Our Institutional Focus

We are committed to three core pillars that define our mission and value to the BCE community.

Community First

Maintaining a robust, global directory of graduates to ensure every BCEian is just a message away from mentorship or opportunity.

Academic Support

Bridging the gap between current students and industry leaders through mentorship, guidance, and financial support programmes.

Institutional Growth

Advocating for the welfare and development of Bhagalpur College of Engineering to ensure its legacy continues for decades to come.
