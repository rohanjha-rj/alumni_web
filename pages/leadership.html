Duties of Office Bearers

The defined responsibilities ensuring the smooth operation and legal compliance of our association.

President

  • Preside at all general and Committee meetings.
  • Responsible for the proper conduct of all association meetings.
  • Exercise the casting vote when necessary.
  • Sign the minutes of each meeting at the time of approval.
  • Sign all cheques on behalf of the Association in conjunction with the General Secretary and Treasurer.
  • Supervise the work of other office bearers from time to time.

Vice President

  • Deputize for the President during his/her absence.
  • Support executive initiatives and leadership coordination.

General Secretary

  • Conduct Association business in accordance with established rules.
  • Responsible for all correspondence and maintaining non-financial documents.
  • Record proceedings of all meetings and maintain a detailed membership register.
  • Sign all cheques on behalf of the Association with the President and Treasurer.
  • File annual returns with the Registrar of Societies within 60 days of the AGM.

Treasurer

  • Responsible for all finances and financial transactions of the Association.
  • Maintain accurate accounts and financial records.
  • Sign all cheques on behalf of the Association with the President and Secretary.
  • Oversee the annual auditing of association accounts.

Joint Office Bearers

  • Joint Secretary: Assist the Secretary and act in his/her absence.
  • Joint Treasurer: Assist the Treasurer in financial oversight and management.

Executive Committee

  • Carry out duties as directed by the President or the Association.
  • Work towards specific initiatives to fulfill the objectives of the association.
  • Financial advisors provide specialized guidance on welfare fund management.
